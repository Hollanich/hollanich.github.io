Fashion Show

    Industrial Design + Fashion

    Two friends of mine, Abby Beuse and Eileen Moores, teamed up with me to create two garments for the ISU Fashion Show competition. Both garments, a womenswear tent dress and an activewear mens outfit, were accepted into the show. Models wore the outfits on a runway for a crowd of about 2,500 people.

    Both garments were made using the same design concept. The concept was to two-tone layer materials on top of each other. The viewer is able to see through to the back material due to laser cut windows cut into the outer material. This two-tonedness is emphasized through the usage of a matte outer layer and a reflective inner layer.
