Paduak Electric Guitar

    About the Guitar

    This is a first attempt at making an electric guitar. Various high quality woods such as padauk, hickory and instrument-grade curly maple were used in its construction. The body, neck and head of the guitar are completely custom made from scratch.

    The guitar is designed to form around the leg and torso of the player when sitting. The body was made thin around the pickups to ergonomically improve user access to the playing area.
