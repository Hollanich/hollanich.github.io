Details about the Polypropylene Lamp

Designed with shadow, light quality and manufacturing feasibility in mind, this lighting unit allows for flatpacking and simple, non-obtrusive hanging.

The Brief

Design a lighting unit using only one sheet of polypropylene plastic, cord, socket and lightbulb. All wiring needed to be done by hand.

The Idea

To use the cord as a structural element, connecting the entire system. Use the body of the shade mixed with offcuts from the plastic sheeting as packaging.

The Solution

Hang the lightbulb in the polypropylene and wrap it around a T-shaped top on the shade in order to hold everything together. Use red, fabric cord to display the connection point.
